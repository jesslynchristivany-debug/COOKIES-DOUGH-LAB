OUR STORY

From home kitchen to many stores

Our story starts with quality, craft, and a personal touch.

Dough Lab had very humble beginnings, starting out in the home kitchen of our founder Hana Makarim in December 2017. From there, the brand quickly expanded to a fully professional kitchen, creating a delicious spread of hand-crafted cookies using only the highest quality and natural ingredients. In 2019, Dough Lab opened its first shop in Senopati, Kebayoran Baru. Today, Dough Lab has grown to 19 locations across Jakarta, Bogor, Bali, and Surabaya. We also offer soft serve ice cream and coffee pairings. A unique characteristic of Dough Lab is its “cookie personalities”, inspired by strong women around the founder.
